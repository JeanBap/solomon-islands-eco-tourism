About

Work. Learn. Restore.

A small, considered marketplace pairing affluent purpose-driven travellers with verified Solomon Islands eco lodges and active marine conservation projects.

What we do

We curate 4 to 7 day premium-eco journeys in the Solomon Islands and across the Coral Triangle. Each journey pairs a stay at a vetted eco lodge with hands-on engagement at an active marine conservation project: reef survey, coral planting, mangrove planting, ranger ride-along, marine debris removal. Every booking includes a per-booking impact report.

Why this exists

The Solomon Islands sit at the heart of the Coral Triangle, the global epicentre of marine biodiversity. The country has one of the most distinctive community-led conservation models in the Pacific, anchored by the Tetepare Descendants Association and the Arnavon Community Marine Conservation Area. There is a growing audience of affluent purpose-driven travellers who want to connect their travel spend to verifiable conservation outcomes. We exist to make that connection direct, transparent, and scalable.

The model

30% platform

Operations, curation, payments, concierge, technology, partner programmes.

12% hotel margin

Direct margin to the lodge above accommodation pass-through. Helps premium-eco lodges stay sustainable.

20% conservation share

Of experience fees. Direct payment to the named project partner with a per-booking impact report.

Pass-through

Accommodation and inter-island transport at cost. No hidden margin.

Verified partners

Every lodge and conservation programme on our interactive atlas is vetted against published criteria. The Solomon Islands flagships include Uepi Island Resort on the Marovo Lagoon barrier reef, Tetepare Descendants Association, Arnavon Community Marine Conservation Area, and Coral Sea Foundation.
