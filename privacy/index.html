Privacy policy.

Plain-English summary of what we collect, why, how it is stored, who it is shared with, and your rights.

Last updated: 2 May 2026.

What we collect

If you submit the inquiry form on the Plan your journey page, we collect: full name, email address, country, party size, destination interest, length and timing, conservation focus preferences, budget band, and any free-text notes you choose to share. We collect this only because you submit it.

We use standard server logs that record IP address, user agent, and request paths for security and debugging. We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

Why we collect it

To prepare and send tailored journey itineraries to you. To respond to questions. To keep records of bookings if you choose to proceed.

Who we share it with

Lodges and conservation project partners only when, and only as much as, is needed to deliver an itinerary you have asked for. Our payments processor (Stripe) and our transactional email provider (Resend) when you book and travel. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

How long we keep it

Inquiry data: up to 24 months from your last contact with us, or until you ask us to delete it. Booking and tax records: as required by Australian and UK accounting law (typically 7 years).

Your rights

You can ask us to correct or delete your data at any time. Email hello@solomonislandseco.example. We respond within 30 days.

Cookies

The site uses essential cookies only (no advertising cookies, no third-party trackers). We do not need a banner under EU GDPR for essential-only cookies, but if that changes we will display one.

Data location

Static site assets are hosted on GitHub Pages. Form submissions sent via mailto: reach our standard email infrastructure. If you book, payment data is processed by Stripe.

Changes to this policy

We update this page when our practices change. Material changes are dated above and announced on the site.

Contact

Privacy enquiries: hello@solomonislandseco.example.
