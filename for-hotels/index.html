The economics

Standard agency rate plus a 12% margin.

Pricing transparency is built into everything we do, including with partners. Of a typical $2,500 package we sell, accommodation pass-through is paid to you at your published rate. On top of that we add a 12% margin paid to you in exchange for hosting the experience.

For the first cohort of bookings, we waive our take rate entirely. You receive 100% of the accommodation pass-through plus the 12% margin on the first five bookings, with no platform fee deducted on the hotel side.

Things partners ask.

Is this exclusive?

No. You keep every existing channel. We do not undercut your direct booking rates and we do not require any kind of channel exclusivity. We are an additional, premium-only stream.

What happens if a guest cancels?

The platform applies a published refund ladder: 60+ days 90% refund to guest, 30 to 60 days 50%, 15 to 30 days 25%, under 15 days 0% except medical or force majeure. You are paid your accommodation share according to the same ladder, so we share cancellation risk in line with the guest's cancellation timing.

How are payouts handled?

Weekly reconciliation report by email, payout in USD via Wise by default, bank transfer on request. Currency conversion is at the live mid-market rate at time of payout. We can pay in SBD if your operating account is local.

Do you require photo or marketing rights?

We use your existing public photos and copy unless you provide new material. We never publish anything about your property without sign-off. Co-branded campaigns are opt-in only.

What happens if my team cannot host a particular week?

You can decline any week. Our calendar is built around your availability, not the other way around. We never confirm a booking on a week you have not opened.

Who is the right contact at your end?

Yanni Papoutsis personally, for the first 18 months. After that we will introduce a partner-relations lead, with you as one of the first partners they meet.
